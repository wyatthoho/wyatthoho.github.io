Paramore is an American rock band formed in 
Franklin, Tennessee, in 2004. Since 2017, the 
band's lineup includes lead vocalist Hayley 
Williams, lead guitarist Taylor York, and 
drummer Zac Farro. Williams and Farro are 
founding members of the group, while York, 
a high school friend of the original lineup, 
joined in 2007.
